What is Plesk
-------------

Plesk is a [hosting panel](https://www.plesk.com) with simple and secure web server, website and web apps management tools. It is specially designed to help web professionals manage web, DNS, mail and other services through a comprehensive and user-friendly GUI. Plesk is about intelligently managing servers, apps, websites and hosting businesses, on both traditional and cloud hosting.

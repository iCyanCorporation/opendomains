Margaret River Tours
====================

**The Ultimate in Margaret River Wine, Beer & Food Experiences**

Star Class Tours Margaret River
-------------------------------

Our Margaret River tours cater for everyone here at Star Class Tours. Choose from an exclusive set tour with fine dining and wine tasting on our [luxury winery tours](/luxury-winery-tours/). On the other hand, our [private charters](/private-charter/) allow you to choose where you and your guests wish to kick back and take in the surroundings. And for the little humans (and big ones alike), let’s make a cheeky visit to Bahen & Co Chocolate Maker on our [family daycation](/family-daycation/) Tour.

Our Margaret River tours are suitable for individuals, groups, or private charters and can include children on our Family Daycation Tour. We offer daily [brewery tours](/brewery-tours-margaret-river/) and [wine tours](/wine-tours-margaret-river/) with **free pickup** from Margaret River, Busselton, Dunsborough, Yallingup, and anywhere in between. We can also provide a [transport](/margaret-river-transport/) service from Augusta, please contact us to discuss further. Please note, our pick-up & drop-off times will vary depending on the chosen tour.

Here at Star Class Tours Margaret River, we choose only the **very best venues**, not only for their products but their service and inclusiveness for anybody and everybody that wants to visit the beautiful South-West of Western Australia. Star Class Tours aligns itself with venues and experiences that share our values of kindness and a big smile for anyone who walks through the door.

It is our mission to provide our customers with an experience, filling you not only with delicious food and wine, but knowledge to impress friends when sharing a bottle from the everlasting Margaret River region. Ultimately… Margaret River Tours which are well above the ordinary.
